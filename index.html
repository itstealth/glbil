Eligibility Criteria

Your Legal Journey Starts Here

Check if you meet the requirements and take your first step toward a prestigious legal career with GL Bajaj.

B.A. LL.B. / B.Com. LL.B. Minimum 50% marks in Class 10+2 from any recognised board
LL.B. (3 Years) Graduation from any stream with minimum 50% aggregate marks
Age Limit As per Bar Council of India norms (20 yrs for 5-yr / 30 yrs for 3-yr)
  • 01
    Academic Qualification Candidates must have passed 10+2 (for integrated programmes) or Graduation (for LL.B.) from a recognised university or board.
  • 02
    Minimum Marks A minimum of 50% aggregate marks in the qualifying examination. SC/ST candidates may be eligible for relaxation as per government norms.
  • 03
    Entrance Exam Admission based on merit in CLAT / CUET-UG / State Law Entrance Tests or GLBIL's institutional entrance assessment.
  • 04
    Documents Required 10th & 12th Mark Sheets, Transfer Certificate, Migration Certificate, Character Certificate, and recent passport-size photographs.
  • 05
    Age Criteria As mandated by the Bar Council of India. Applicants must not exceed the maximum age prescribed for the respective programme.
