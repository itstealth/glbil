About the Programme

Where Legal Knowledge Meets Real-World Mastery

GL Bajaj Institute of Law has spent 15 years producing lawyers, judges, and legal scholars who define standards across India's judicial and corporate landscape. Affiliated with AKTU and approved by the Bar Council of India, every programme here is built not just to pass exams — but to forge careers.

Our philosophy is simple: law is not memorised, it is practised. From day one, students argue cases in our state-of-the-art Moot Court Hall, draft contracts under senior advocates, and serve communities through our Legal Aid Clinic.

The Difference That Defines You

Not every law school is the same. Here's what makes 15 years of excellence stand apart.

World-Class Moot Court

Our Moot Court Hall replicates a real High Court — complete with witness box, judges' bench, and full AV. Students have won 50+ national and international competitions, including the Philip C. Jessup International Moot.

Practitioner Faculty

Every professor has argued in court. Our panel includes sitting High Court advocates, retired judges, and IIM-trained legal scholars — not just academics.

92% Placement Rate

Digital Law Library

50,000+ volumes plus full access to Manupatra, SCC Online, Westlaw, and Lexis Nexis — the same databases used by India's top law firms.
